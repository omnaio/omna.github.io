OMNA simplifies multi-channel retailing by automating inventory synchronisation, order management and product listing using Shopify.

With OMNA, you can easily sync your Shopify store with these popular marketplaces, saving you time and effort while expanding your reach to new customers. Our platform also offers advanced features to optimize sales and grow your business. Start simplifying your multi-channel retailing with OMNA today.

Sync Shopify Inventory With Marketplaces

- Keep your marketplace products' inventory synced with Shopify

- Automatically map existing marketplace products with your Shopify product

- Option to easily re-map products

- Supports multiple marketplace accounts

- Supports multiple stock location mapping

Sync Marketplace Orders With Shopify

- Import orders from all connected marketplaces to Shopify for consolidated order fulfillment

- Print invoices, shipping labels, and packing slips within the Shopify admin

- Use Shopify reporting to get a complete overview of sales and trends across all channels and marketplace accounts
